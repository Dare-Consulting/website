Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At DARE Management Consultancy Services, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name and contact details (email, phone) when you fill out our contact form
  • Service preferences and areas of interest
  • Messages and inquiries you send to us

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • General location (country/region)
  • Pages you visit on our website
  • Time and date of your visit

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Send you information about our training and consulting programs
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations
Important: We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Secure SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel only
  • Secure data storage practices

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with relevant authorities

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

Email: coach@dareconsulting.group
Website: www.dareconsulting.group

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.
